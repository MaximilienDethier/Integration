How to get Started

  1. Create your account!

    You just need to enter your email, a nickname and a password, and that's it!

    You will get a confirmation mail on the address you entered: valid your account and you're ready to start!

  2. Discover new stories

    Devour new stories, discover new worlds, and make sure you don't miss anything by following your favourite authors!

    Browse all the stories here and keep the track of your favourite stories on your feed

  3. Create a new story

    A novel you'd like to share with the world? Or maybe a super webcomic only waiting to be read?
    Start a new Story!

  4. Share it!

    Show your work to your friends, it's easy!
    You can share it on both Twitter and Facebook, to make sure nobody misses your last update!
